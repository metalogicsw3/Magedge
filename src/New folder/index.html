What is the MagEdge wristband?

The MagEdge wristband is a waterproof, saltwater-resistant drowning prevention device with built-in SOS alerts and GPS tracking. It helps resorts keep guests safe in the water and on property.

How does the SOS feature work?

When a guest presses the SOS button, the wristband instantly sends their exact GPS location to resort staff or lifeguards via our technology — no cellular network required.

Is the wristband comfortable for guests to wear?

Yes. The MagEdge wristband is lightweight, discreet, and designed for all-day comfort, making it ideal for both adults and children at resorts, beaches, and ski lodges.

Can MagEdge work in areas without cellular coverage?

Absolutely. Unlike traditional safety tools, our wristband uses specific connectivity, ensuring reliable emergency communication even in remote or open-water environments.

How do resorts benefit from using MagEdge?

Resorts gain peace of mind with real-time emergency response, reduced liability risks, and stronger reputations for guest safety — all while helping people when it matters most.

Where can MagEdge be used besides sea resorts?

In addition to beaches and hotels, MagEdge is perfect for ski resorts, marinas, cruise lines, yachts, and adventure sports, offering versatile protection in any environment.
